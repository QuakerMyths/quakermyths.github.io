This site was started by a couple of Liberal Friends in the Eastern US who noticed a need for an FAQ of sorts. The plan started as "let's have business cards we can hand out that say things like:

  • Quakers aren't Amish
  • We use computers
  • We don't dress funny…usually"

There are too many to fit on one business card though, so here we have a much larger site, and we figure we can put a few basics with a "learn more at QuakerMythBusters.org" note at the bottom.

The site has been opened up to edits and improvements on GitHub.
